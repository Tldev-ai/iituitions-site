Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is the free assessment an exam?⌄

No — it’s not a test. Our IIT mentor personally interacts with your child, teaches a small topic, and asks conceptual & numerical questions to understand their strengths, gaps, and learning style.

2. Who are the mentors at iiTuitions?⌄

Only IIT/NIT alumni and senior faculty with 8–12 years of experience teach here. No junior or part-time teachers.

3. How are classes conducted?⌄

Every session is 1-on-1 — just your child and the mentor. Sessions are online (Zoom/Meet) with both cameras on. You get daily WhatsApp updates and full transparency.

4. What if we’re not satisfied with the mentor?⌄

You can switch your faculty anytime. We also have a “Progress Guarantee” — if promised milestones aren’t met, you’re eligible for a full refund.

5. What kind of students benefit the most?⌄

Students in Grade 11 & 12 who are preparing for IIT JEE Mains & Advanced, especially those who:

  • Struggle to clear doubts in large coaching batches
  • Feel underconfident before exams
  • Want personalized, IIT-level problem-solving support
